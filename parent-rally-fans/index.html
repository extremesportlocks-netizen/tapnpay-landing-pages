Q: What is TapNPay+?+

TapNPay+ is a fan payment platform. You join for $50 a year, earn ~4% cash back every time you pay at 300+ national brands using Digital Gift Cards — and your program earns a portion of that cash back too.

Q: Does it cost me anything extra?+

No! You pay the exact same price you'd normally pay. The cash back is generated from the digital gift card transaction — there's zero extra cost to you.

Q: How do I earn cash back?+

Open the app, enter your purchase amount, receive a digital gift card instantly on your phone, and scan it to pay at checkout. Cash back activates the moment the transaction completes.

Q: How does my program earn?+

Your program earns a portion of the overall cash back your payments generate through TapNPay+ — automatically, every time you pay.

Q: What brands are available?+

300+ national brands including grocery, restaurants, retail, travel, entertainment, and more. Nike, Dick's, Starbucks, Uber, Chipotle, DoorDash, Home Depot — brands you already use every day.
