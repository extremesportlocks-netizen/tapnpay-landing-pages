Does it cost extra to support my athlete?+

No! You shop at the same stores you already use. TapNPay+ generates cash back for you and NIL earnings for your athlete from the same transaction. Zero extra cost.

How do I earn cash back?+

Purchase digital gift cards through the TapNPay+ app at 300+ national brands. You get an average of 4% instant cash back on every purchase. Cash back can be applied to future purchases or redeemed by check.

How does my athlete earn from my purchases?+

When you register and select your athlete, all your purchase activity through TapNPay+ automatically generates NIL earnings for them. The more you shop, the more they earn.

Can I use TapNPay+ with sales and coupons?+

Yes! Use all your coupons, promo codes, and store sales first, then pay with a TapNPay+ digital gift card to stack cash back on the already-discounted price. You can even use Rakuten alongside TapNPay+ for double rewards.

How do I redeem my cash back?+

Two ways: apply it toward any merchant's digital gift card at checkout, or request a check mailed to your address. Cash back is instant — not quarterly like other platforms.
