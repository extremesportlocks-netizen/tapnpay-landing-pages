Your Compensation — Two Streams

Stream 1 — Membership Commission

You earn 70% of every $50 fan membership attributed to your efforts and engagement.

$50 membership = $35.00 commission. All members receive identical platform access.

Stream 2 — DGC Cash Back Performance Royalty

You earn 10% of the overall cash back your fans generate on Digital Gift Card purchases through TapNPay+. Every time your fans pay with the app, you earn a portion of that cash back — ongoing, for as long as your fans stay active.

Your compensation is earned through your documented promotional activity. It is not tied to your athletic performance or school enrollment.

How You Stay Active — One Simple Rule

Complete a minimum of 1 Share Activation per rolling 30-day period using the TapNPay+ in-app Share button to remain visible in the athlete dropdown and eligible to earn.

Active (1+ shares in 30 days): Visible in dropdown. Eligible to earn.
Paused (0 shares in 30 days): Removed from dropdown. Not eligible to earn.

One tap of the Share button reactivates a Paused account immediately. Your share count and status are visible in real time in your athlete dashboard.
